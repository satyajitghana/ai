MockLab generates pixel-perfect social mockups across 21+ platforms — social posts (X,
LinkedIn, Instagram post/story, Reddit, Threads, YouTube, TikTok), chat messages
(WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, Discord, iMessage, Snapchat), and Gmail. Edit on the left,
watch the live preview on the right, download as 2× PNG.

Everything is configurable: verified badges, reactions, timestamps, read receipts, media
uploads, and per-message editing for the chat mockups — all with dark/light theming for
both the app and every mockup. It's a deceptively deep UI project: each platform is its
own little pixel-accurate design system to reproduce.
